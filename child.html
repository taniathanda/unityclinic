Child & Adolescent Health

UHC offers a wide range of women’s Health Services encompassing all seasons of a woman’s life. From talking to a pubescent teen about her periods and HPV vaccines to a young woman about contraceptive options and sexual health, family planning, fertility awareness, Pap smears, menopause and osteoporosis for more mature women.
We believe in empowering women and patients to actively participate in their health to improve health outcomes and quality of life. Our priority is to promote healthy living through preventative health, increasing awareness of risk factors, and early detection and intervention.
In addition, our doctors use evidenced based practice to ensure the most up-to-date methods, translating research findings into patient care practices and clinical decision-making.
