Targeted Medical Care

Welcome to UHC, a renowned healthcare provider in Singapore since 1998. We’re dedicated to offering a comprehensive range of services, including general practice, men’s health, and vaccinations, all tailored to meet the needs of our diverse community. Whether you’re a local resident, an expat, or new to Singapore, our multi-lingual team of experienced doctors is here to provide you with exceptional care.

We see patients and families from all over the world and we are experienced in following, integrating and customising multiple international vaccination schedules. If you want to continue on the vaccination schedule from your home country then we can tailor the schedule to suit.
