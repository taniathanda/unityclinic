Our doctors are internationally trained GPs, Family Physicians and Paediatricians that provide medical care for the whole family. Our doctors offer all aspects of acute and chronic primary health care. We also have various minor surgical procedures and in-clinic treatments available at our clinics. In addition, we have dedicated treatment rooms that are fully equipped.

Our Registered Nurses and support staff are trained to provide an array of services to meet your healthcare needs.

Services include:

• Blood taking and specimen collection for pathology tests.
• Dispensing of medication – we stock a comprehensive range of medications and vaccines for convenience.
• Administration of infusions and other in-clinic procedures.
• Coordinating imaging services and specialist appointments.
